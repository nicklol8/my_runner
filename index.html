About

Thanks for playing DonutDash!

This game is all about Troy, a boy who dreams of donuts all day and night. Here we find him mid dream, surrounded by donuts, running on cakes. What a sweet dream to have! Play by using the spacebar or click to jump! You can do that twice! When you are happy with your high score, submit it to the leaderboard! Happy Dashing!
